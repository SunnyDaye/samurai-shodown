Samurai Shodown  BETA

Samurai Shodown is a fighting game built on Phaser Javascript game framework. All images are copyrighted by SNK. It's a work in progress so please expect some funny bugs in the game.

You are a samarai feeling down because you feel that you are not meant to be a real samarai. You've recently have faced adversary and have lost constant battles. You think to yourself, "Am I good enough? When will I be able to prove myself!". Suddenly, an elder samarai approaches you with a challenge. "Your biggest enemy is youself.", the elder says. Suddenly, you spawn into another world ridden with the smell of battle. You look across from and notice your opponent. The opponent has many of the same features as you do. Matter of fact, the opponent looks just like you except evil. "My greatest enemy is myself", you think to yourself. It's time to prove yourself worthy.

    If using a keyboard, the keyboard layout is below:

  • A: Walk backward
  • S: Crouch
  • D: Walk forward
  • W: Jump
  • I: Medium slash
  • O: Medium kick
  • P: Heavy slash

    • The movement buttons (e.g. ASDW) acts like a joystick. To do a quarter circle forward motion, press S > D. To do an uppercut motion, press D > S > D. To block an attack, press the button away from the opponent's direction. To perform a critical art, press E twice.
